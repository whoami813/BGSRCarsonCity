# Boardgame Setup Randomizer Carson City : Big Box
🤠 Carson City: Big Box – Setup Randomiser

Age of vibe coding! Carson City Setup Randomiser is a digital companion for the legendary board game Carson City: Big Box by Xavier Georges. Stop fumbling with dice and bags—get your frontier town built in seconds.

🌐 Launch the Web App

🌵 Features

This app saves you time from dice rolling and giving back your time:

🗺️ Dynamic Map Generation: Instantly places the City Centre and 9 Mountains on an 8x8 grid. It even handles the initial road placement on the parcel edges!

🏗️ Building Track Randomiser: Automatically draws the starting buildings for the $5, $6, and $8 slots from the official "bag" distribution (excluding Ranches and Mines).

🎲 Turn Order Track: Choose from 6 iconic player colours (Orange, Green, Blue, Yellow, Brown, Red). The app shuffles active players into a horizontal Round 1 turn order.
